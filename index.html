About Us

We are a husband and wife team passionate about sharing halal-friendly experiences across Tokyo and beyond. Having lived in Japan for many years — and my wife her entire life — we know the best spots that often remain hidden from visitors.

Our goal is to make travel easier for Muslim travelers by guiding you to authentic halal restaurants, prayer spaces, and memorable cultural sights.
